In a future world where Earth faces extreme resource scarcity, humanity has built a single remaining survival base. Only individuals who are highly intelligent or hold significant power are allowed to live inside the base. Ordinary people are abandoned across the ruined Earth.

The solar system has collapsed, and the distances between celestial bodies have become too vast to travel, making interplanetary escape impossible.

A scientist later discovers a method based on quantum mechanics that allows present humans to connect with people from the past. Through a temporary form of consciousness “possession,” individuals can inhabit historical figures and collect resources that can be preserved for long-term survival. These resources are then stored in specific ways so they can be retrieved in the present.

However, this method is not a permanent solution.

---

## Chapters

### 1. Medieval Europe
- **Host identity:** Farmer  
- **Environment:** Villages affected by plague  
- **Resources:** Dried meat, wheat  
- **Storage location:** Forest cellar  
- **Challenge:** Limited entry time due to plague, avoid witches searching the village  

---

### 2. Han Dynasty (China)
- **Host identity:** Merchant  
- **Environment:** Markets and warehouses  
- **Resources:** Preserved foods (pickled goods)  
- **Storage location:** Warehouse system  
- **Challenge:** Complete collection within time limit and avoid inspection or pursuit  

---

### 3. Ancient Persia
- **Host identity:** Soldier  
- **Environment:** Royal palace and desert regions  
- **Resources:** Spices  
- **Storage location:** Hidden desert locations  
- **Challenge:** Avoid detection from witches and palace guards within the time limit  

---

Each chapter has a strict time limit. Players must collect required food resources in the map, avoid being tracked or captured, and successfully store the items in designated locations to complete the mission.

This is the current core game concept.
